# PADI Divemaster Exam B - Flashcards

## Question 1
**True or False.** The two characteristics that divers expect of a PADI Divemaster are professionalism and role-model behavior.
- [ ] True
- [ ] False
**Answer:** True
**Feedback:** Nailed it! 🔨 You are the gold standard on the boat.

## Question 2
Why do certified divers choose to dive with a PADI Divemaster when they can dive unsupervised?
- [ ] a. A divemaster can handle logistics, and make diving more convenient and fun.
- [ ] b. Divemasters take care of every detail of individual divers' plans.
- [ ] c. Boat dives are only possible with a divemaster.
- [ ] d. All of the above.
**Answer:** a
**Feedback:** You make diving a breeze! 🌴 Handling logistics equals maximum fun for the guests.

## Question 3
Which of these characteristics does a good role-model divemaster have?
- [ ] a. Has excellent dive skills and practices responsible diving.
- [ ] b. Is equipped with well maintained and up-to-date dive gear.
- [ ] c. Has good fitness for diving.
- [ ] d. All of the above.
**Answer:** d
**Feedback:** All of the above! 🦸♂️ Skills, gear, and fitness make the ultimate dive superhero.

## Question 4
The professional and ethical behavior expected of you as a PADI Member is listed in the ________ found in your PADI Instructor Manual.
- [ ] a. License Agreement
- [ ] b. Liability Release
- [ ] c. Code of Practice
- [ ] d. Active Status
**Answer:** c
**Feedback:** The Code of Practice! 📖 Memorize it, live it, breathe it. 

## Question 5
**True or False.** As a PADI Divemaster, you have the responsibility to adhere to PADI Standards when assisting with or conducting PADI programs.
- [ ] True
- [ ] False
**Answer:** True
**Feedback:** Standards are standard for a reason! 📏 Keep it safe and keep it by the book.

## Question 6
**True or False.** Having a large knowledge and experience base makes it easier for an expert to identify cause-and-effect relationships when dealing with problems and to better create solutions.
- [ ] True
- [ ] False
**Answer:** True
**Feedback:** Big brain energy! 💡 Experience helps you connect the dots faster when things go wrong.

## Question 7
As a divemaster, what is your role in dive planning for a group of certified divers?
- [ ] a. Assess the equipment needs for each individual in the group.
- [ ] b. Take on the responsibility of all dive planning details for everyone.
- [ ] c. Provide broad planning information that divers use to plan their individual dives.
- [ ] d. All of the above.
**Answer:** c
**Feedback:** Give them the canvas, let them paint! 🎨 You provide the briefing, they plan their dive.

## Question 8
When evaluating dive conditions, what factors do you need to consider?
- [ ] a. Visibility
- [ ] b. Wind, rain and temperature
- [ ] c. Currents and tides
- [ ] d. All of the above.
**Answer:** d
**Feedback:** Everything matters! 🌪️ The weather, the water, and everything in between.

## Question 9
As a dive supervisor, one effective way to plan an upcoming dive is to:
- [ ] a. always follow the same dive plan for every dive.
- [ ] b. think through the dive from start to finish.
- [ ] c. ask an instructor to provide dive details.
- [ ] d. None of the above.
**Answer:** b
**Feedback:** Play the tape in your head! 🎬 Thinking it through helps you anticipate logistical hiccups.

## Question 10
The primary purpose of diver accounting procedures is to:
- [ ] a. make certain that all divers have a buddy.
- [ ] b. obtain information needed to plan repetitive dives.
- [ ] c. track divers in and out of the water - making sure all divers have returned after a dive.
- [ ] d. check whether each diver has the proper equipment for a dive.
**Answer:** c
**Feedback:** Don't leave anyone behind! 🚤 Accounting is literally counting heads so you don't drive away without someone!
